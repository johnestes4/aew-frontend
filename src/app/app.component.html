Elite Power Rankings is a fan project not affiliated with or endorsed by All Elite Wrestling.

Power levels are calculated based on a modified Elo rating system, with additional modifiers for winning and losing streaks, and a decay system where a match's contribution to a power level decreases over time.

Elite Power Rankings is in beta. Power calculation is regularly rebalanced, and power levels may shift between shows. Trios rankings are coming soon.

If you have questions or encounter bugs, you can reach me on Twitter @JohnDudebro.
